1. Start.
2. Declare variables power and totalLoad.
3. Initialize totalLoad = 0.
4. While totalLoad is less than or equal to 3000 W:
  -Input the appliance power rating.
  -Add the power rating to totalLoad.
  -Display the current total load.
5. If totalLoad exceeds 3000 W:
  -Display "Load limit exceeded. Do not add more appliances."
6. Display the final total load.
7. Stop.
