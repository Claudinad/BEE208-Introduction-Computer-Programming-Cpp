1. Start.
2. Declare variables temperature, total, and count.
3. Initialize total = 0 and count = 0.
4. Use a for loop to accept 10 temperature readings.
5. For each reading:
  -Input the temperature.
 -If the temperature is less than 0°C:
    -Display "Invalid reading."
    -Use continue to skip the reading.
  -Otherwise:
    -Add the temperature to total.
    -Increase count by 1.
6. After the loop:
  -If count > 0, calculate the average temperature.
  -Display the average.
7. Stop.
