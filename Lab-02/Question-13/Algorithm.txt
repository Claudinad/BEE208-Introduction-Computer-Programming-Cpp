1. Start.
2. Declare a variable voltage.
3. Use a for loop to accept up to 10 battery voltage readings.
4. For each reading:
  -Input the battery voltage.
  -If the voltage is less than 10.5 V:
    -Display "Low battery detected. Test stopped."
    -Use break to terminate the loop.
  -Otherwise:
    -Display "Battery voltage normal."
5. Stop.
