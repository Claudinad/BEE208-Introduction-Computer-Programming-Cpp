1. Start.
2. Declare variables for choice, voltage, current, power, resistance, time, and energy.
3. Display the menu:
  -Calculate Power
  -Calculate Resistance
  -Calculate Energy
  -Exit
4. Accept the user's choice.
5. Use a switch statement to perform the selected operation:
  -Calculate Power = Voltage × Current
  -Calculate Resistance = Voltage ÷ Current
  -Calculate Energy = Power × Time
  -Exit the program
6. Repeat the menu using a do...while loop until the user selects option 4.
7. Stop.
